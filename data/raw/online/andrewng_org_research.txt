Research
Andrew Ng has authored or co-authored over 200 research papers in machine learning, robotics, computer vision, and natural language processing. His work spans foundational contributions to deep learning, reinforcement learning, and data-centric AI — from the Stanford AI Lab and the Google Brain project to applied research at Baidu and beyond.
